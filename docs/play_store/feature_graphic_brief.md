# Feature graphic brief — Cow Bull Quest

Status: **completed brief only. No graphic is generated by this
document** — producing the actual 1024×500 asset is a separate, later
design task.

## Specification

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Exact size | **1024×500 px**, PNG or JPEG per current Play Console feature-graphic requirements (reconfirm the exact format/size limit in Play Console before export) |
| Identity | Cow Bull Quest — title text optional (see taglines below), but if present must read exactly "Cow Bull Quest" |
| Icon/emblem | Use only the approved launcher-icon emblem from `assets/branding/cow_bull_quest_icon.png` (per `branding_guide.md`) — no redrawn or alternate mark |
| Background | Navy/royal-blue, drawn from the approved dark-theme palette: `#0B1026` (darkest navy) through `#1A2650` (elevated navy/royal-blue), matching the app's splash/dark-theme background |
| Accents | Gold (`#FFC33D`, brand primary) and cyan (`#2EC6FF`, tertiary) only, per `branding_guide.md`'s final palette — no other accent colors |
| Tagline | Optional, concise, factual (see three options below) |
| Composition | Minimal: emblem + optional short tagline, generous negative space — not a collage of screenshots or UI chrome |
| Safe area | Keep all essential content (emblem, tagline) within the central safe area, since Play Store may crop or overlay UI elements near the edges on some surfaces |

## Explicitly excluded from the graphic

- No Play Store badge ("Get it on Google Play" or similar) — Play inserts
  its own store chrome; do not duplicate it.
- No ratings, star ratings, or review-count callouts.
- No awards, "Editor's Choice," or similar badges — none have been earned
  and none may be implied.
- No claims for unsupported features (no "multiplayer," "leaderboards,"
  "AI," etc. — see `branding_guide.md` "Prohibited uses").
- No company branding of any kind, from any unrelated company — see
  `branding_guide.md`'s "Prohibited uses" section for the full rule this
  brief follows.

## Three optional taglines

Pick at most one; each is concise and describes only what the app
actually does.

1. "Crack the code. Bulls and Cows clues."
2. "A word to guess. Clues to follow."
3. "Guess smarter with every clue."

## Handoff

This brief describes composition and constraints only. The actual asset
should be produced by whoever executes visual design work, using the
approved icon source and palette referenced above, and reviewed against
this brief's exclusion list before upload.
