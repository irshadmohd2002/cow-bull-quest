# Tester instructions — Cow Bull Quest closed testing

Status: **completed draft**, ready to send to testers once the opt-in
link exists (see `closed_testing_plan.md`).

## Joining the test

1. You'll receive a private **opt-in link** by email or through the
   tester Google Group. Open it on the Android device you want to test
   with.
2. Tap **"Become a tester"** (or the equivalent button shown on that
   page) and follow the on-screen instructions.
3. You'll be redirected to the Cow Bull Quest listing on Google Play.
   Tap **Install**.
4. **Do not share this opt-in link publicly** (e.g. on social media or a
   public forum) unless the developer has explicitly said the test is
   open to wider sharing — closed testing links are meant for the invited
   tester group only.

## Installing and updating

- Install directly from the Play Store page you're redirected to after
  opting in — do not sideload an APK from any other source.
- When a new test build is released, Play should prompt you to update
  automatically, or you can check for an update from the app's Play Store
  page. Please update promptly so feedback reflects the current build.
- Note the app's **version** (visible on the Play Store listing page, or
  ask the developer if unsure) before reporting anything — this helps
  match your feedback to the correct build.

## Minimum test scenarios

Please try all of the following at least once:

1. **Theme persistence** — in Settings, choose Light, then Dark, then
   Follow system. Force-close and reopen the app after each choice and
   confirm the theme you picked is still applied.
2. **Multiple word lengths and difficulties** — play at least one
   complete game (win or lose) at each of the three word lengths (4, 5,
   and 6 letters) and try more than one difficulty (Easy, Common, Hard).
3. **Statistics** — after a few completed games, open Statistics and
   confirm your wins/losses, streaks, and recent games look correct. Try
   the "Clear statistics" action and confirm it asks for confirmation and
   then empties your history.
4. **Force-stop / relaunch behavior** — force-stop the app from Android
   Settings (or swipe it away from Recent Apps) mid-game, then relaunch
   it. Note what happens (e.g. whether you land back on Home, and whether
   your prior statistics/theme are intact).
5. **Text scaling** — in Android Settings → Display → Font size (or
   Accessibility → Font size), try both a small and a large text size,
   then reopen the app and check that text on Home, Game, Rules, Settings,
   and Statistics screens remains readable and doesn't visibly overlap or
   get cut off.

## Reporting an issue

Please use `docs/play_store/tester_feedback_template.md` (or the form the
developer shares alongside these instructions) and include:

- **Device model** (e.g. "Pixel 7a")
- **Android version** (Settings → About phone → Android version)
- **App version** (from the Play Store listing page)
- **Steps to reproduce** — exact taps/inputs, in order
- **Expected result**
- **Actual result**
- **Screenshots or a short screen recording**, if possible — remember not
  to include anything personal in the background of a screen recording

## A note on sharing

This is a **closed** test. The opt-in link and any builds you receive are
for your own testing use — please don't republish screenshots, the APK,
or the opt-in link outside the tester group unless the developer says
otherwise.

Thank you for helping test Cow Bull Quest!
