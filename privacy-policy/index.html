Privacy Policy for Cow Bull Quest

Effective date: 15 July 2026

Cow Bull Quest is developed and published by Md Irshadullah Gharbi. This privacy policy explains how the app handles information.

Information collection

Cow Bull Quest does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app does not require an account and does not use advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting services.

Local app data

The app stores theme preferences and completed-game statistics locally on your device. This information is not sent to the developer or to any external server.

Secret words and guessed words are not stored as part of your saved statistics.

Permissions and device access

Cow Bull Quest does not request access to:

  • Location
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Broad file or storage access

Third-party services

The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, or account services.

Data retention and deletion

Locally stored statistics remain on your device until you clear them within the app, clear the app's storage through Android settings, or uninstall the app.

Children's privacy

Cow Bull Quest is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed toward children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or other users.

Security

Because Cow Bull Quest does not transmit personal data to a developer server, your game preferences and statistics remain within the app's local storage on your device.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change. The updated policy will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
